History

Since its founding in 1990, our company has been offering exciting and memorable adventures to all our customers. Over the past 34 years, we have brought joy to thousands of people, guiding them to explore the most impressive and challenging rivers.

Committed to safety, our guides are highly qualified and we offer experiences tailored to all skill levels. We have grown and evolved, but our mission remains to offer unique adventures that connect people to each other.
